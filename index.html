Welcome to the new OutlandSMP website! Check out our rules and join our Discord for updates.

Vanilla Survival

Pure Minecraft experience with no game-changing plugins. Build, mine, and explore in a 10,000 x 10,000 block world border.

Strategic PvP

PvP is enabled in the Overworld and Nether for intense combat, but keep your guard up - the End is a safe zone from player combat.

Grief Protection

Your builds are safe! Stealing and griefing are strictly prohibited. Focus on building amazing structures without worry.

World Border: 10,000 x 10,000 Blocks

Plenty of space to build, explore, and claim your territory. The border ensures a tight-knit community while offering vast lands to conquer.

Dimension Rules

Overworld

PvP is ENABLED. Watch your back while building and exploring. Form alliances or hunt your enemies.

Nether

PvP is ENABLED. The dangerous dimension just got more intense. Fight for resources and survival.

The End

PvP is DISABLED (Bannable). Safe zone for end-game activities. Violating this rule results in immediate ban.

Server Rules

Critical Rules

  • 1
    No Griefing

    Destroying or altering other players' builds without permission is strictly prohibited and results in permanent ban.

  • 2
    No Stealing

    Taking items from chests, item frames, or players without permission is forbidden. Respect others' property.

  • 3
    End Dimension PvP Ban

    PvP in The End is strictly bannable. The End is designated as a safe zone for dragon fights and end-game activities.

PvP Rules

  • 1
    Overworld & Nether Only

    PvP is only permitted in the Overworld and Nether dimensions. Combat logging is not allowed.

  • 2
    No Spawn Camping

    Do not camp at spawn points or repeatedly kill the same player without giving them a chance to escape.

  • 3
    Fair Fights

    Do not use exploits, hacks, or unfair advantages in PvP. Keep it vanilla and skill-based.

General Conduct

  • 1
    Respect All Players

    No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior. Treat others with respect.

  • 2
    No Cheating

    X-ray, fly hacks, speed hacks, or any client modifications giving unfair advantages are forbidden.

  • 3
    No Lag Machines

    Do not build contraptions designed to crash or lag the server intentionally.

  • 4
    English in Chat

    Please keep public chat in English so moderators can monitor conversations.
