Experience True Minecraft Survival

MistyMC is a pure survival vanilla SMP with a hardcore spirit that brings you back to the roots of what made Minecraft legendary.

No server resets, no pay-to-win, no overpowered kits, no claims - just pure, unadulterated Minecraft survival where your skills, creativity, and determination matter most.

We are committed to being a non-discriminatory, safe community that welcomes players of all backgrounds. We have zero tolerance for hate speech, profanity, harassment, or any form of discrimination. Please read our rules to understand our community standards and ensure everyone can enjoy a positive gaming experience.

Pure Vanilla Experience

  • Experience Minecraft exactly as the developers intended - no game-changing modifications that alter the core survival experience.
  • Authentic survival mechanics
  • No overpowered tools or kits
  • True vanilla gameplay balance

  • No unwanted PvP
